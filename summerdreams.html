A Midsummer Night's Dream

A capsule in Black, Ivory and Silver, hand-crafted from hand-woven diaphanous Tangail cotton, lustrous Madka and Mysore silks and a super-luxurious sexy silk-spandex. Fresh, Fun and Flirtatious accessories Flash in Luscious Colours.

The fabrics and borders are designed by me and custom-crafted exclusively for my atelier by the artisanal Tangail weavers of Bengal. Our textile designs require traditional hand-loom techniques that are no longer commonly used because they are extremely labour intensive. In addition, only the finest cotton and highest quality silk go into the creation of these luxurious weaves. We believe that all the cloth used in this collection is of a quality that is consistent with Jamdani's centuries-old reputation as an imperial fabric.

Featured Accessories: velvet chain bag, bow-tie hairband, hand-made-pom-pom ear-cuff, ear-ring and bangle, hand-woven silk bracelet, crochet slippers with hand-embroidered sequins and/or pom-poms and/or hand-woven silk butterfly-knots
