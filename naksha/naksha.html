Artwear Naksha Skirt

Almost from the first days of my atelier, The Naksha skirt has been my signature piece of artwear, and an evergreen favourite with clients.

As the name suggests, each ArtWear Naksha is a one-of-a-kind piece, unique and exclusive

The perfect fusion of traditional Tangail textile in a modern western silhouette, the Naksha skirt is a multi-paneled, crushed skirt. Made of sheer 100% cotton fabrics, the skirt offers comfort, playfulness and complete freedom of movement. It suits every age group from teenagers (we even have a kids' Naksha) to seniors.

Each Artwear Naksha skirt is cut from a single Tangail / Jamdani sari. The skirt is lined with Voile de Coton, to create our signature light-layer effect.

The waist band is made up of 7 rows of elastic stitched into. It takes a skilled tailor most of a day to craft the waist. The result is a snug, comfortable fit

Sizing

The Naksha is available in 4 lengths, Micro, Mini, Midi and Minuit. Because the waist-band is elastic, it is a free-size garment, fitting a range of waist measurements.
